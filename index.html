About

Me.

Myself Abhay Renjan from Kumbalangi, Kerala. I'm currently pursuing my B-Tech in Computer Science from College of Engineering, Cherthala.

I was that introverted guy who sits in front of the computer the whole day long watching YouTube and nothing else. Tech has always fascinated me. Every new gadget and its features made me feel amazed about how technology is changing rapidly. I was introduced to my first programming language, Python, in high school. I don't know whether it was the easiness of Python or my interest in programming which made me pretty good at it. So I ended up taking B-Tech in Computer Science which I'm not that proud of.

Anyway, the past is something which I don't wanna bother about. So, I'm going with the flow of life, choosing the choices I want, and waiting for what tomorrow has got for me.
